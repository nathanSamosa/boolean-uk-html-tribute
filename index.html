   Introductions   

Milly

Milly is a 9-year-old tabby tortoiseshell.
She is diabetic and needs insulin every morning and evening.
She will never thank you for literally keeping her alive.
Milly is the boss of the house.
Only loves you if you give her snacks.
Will deliberately eat slowly so you give her more snacks to finish her meal.
She could not care less about Storm.

Storm

Storm is a 7-year-old border collie.
He has the attention span of an infant
Storm loves to chase sticks... But he doesn't pick them up.
He literally just stares at the stick on the ground and leaves.
He loves every human. He has zero interest in other dogs.
Other dogs don't feed him.
He is absolutely terrified of Milly.
